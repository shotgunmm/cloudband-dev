CLOUDBAND 3.0 Launch
As service providers adopt network functions virtualization (NFV) and move NFV platforms & applications into production, their focus shifts from feasibility studies to lean operations and reliable service delivery. The CloudBand 3.0 NFV platform has been specifically designed to simplify and de-risk the move from proof-of-concept to production

This new release has been optimized to fully address three major Service Provider requirements:
  1. Production ready: A stable, pre-integrated, high-availability platform with rigid quality assurance processes and automated software regression testing;

  2. Low entry threshold: A ready-to-use solution tailored for initially, single virtual network function (VNF) deployments and scalable from there to support multiple VNFs and large, distributed geographies;

  3. Field-proven openness: CloudBand supports NFV infrastructures and VNFs from Alcatel-Lucent as well as many other vendors
