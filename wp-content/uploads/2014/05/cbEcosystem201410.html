NFV INSIGHT SERIES WHITE PAPER
THE RIGHT SDN - IS RIGHT FOR NFV

To maximize the potential of Network Functions Virtualization (NFV) and open up new opportunities, such as service chaining, the network must be made as dynamic and programmable as the virtualized network functions. SDN is an essential and complementary technology that enhances the advantages of NFV, but the SDN implemented must be tailored to an operator’s specific NFV requirements. The degree of integration between SDN and NFV can vary, bringing different degrees of reward to the service provider. This paper explores the value a carefully-chosen SDN can bring to NFV. It also explores the four stages of integration, which provide increasing levels of operational optimization.

NFV MASHUP SERIES
“Where the Magic Happens” A look at our Cloud Innovation Center and the advancements in 2014. Featuring ecosystem partner Radcom.

November 18, 2014 -- 9:00 AM PST

All NFV ecosystems are not created equal. The CloudBand Ecosystem has access to Alcatel-Lucent’s Cloud Innovation Center located in Naperville, Illinois. This facility founded over two years ago, is our epicenter for NFV Innovation. The CIC is a state of the art Telco grade environment that simulates the inter-workings of a service provider’s data center. It is engineered to simulate the affects that occur when implementing Virtual Network Functions. The CIC works with our ecosystem partners, internal business units and customers to showcase, test, and demonstrate the advancements that can be made by shifting from an IT infrastructure to a Cloud Infrastructure. Joe Tieu will walk us through the various use cases and advancements that have taken place at the CIC over the past year and how Alcatel-Lucent is helping Service Providers push the NFV envelope with this unique lab.
