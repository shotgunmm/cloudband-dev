CLOUDBAND PRODUCT UPDATE
What´s New in the CloudBand 3.0 Release?
In June, a major version of CloudBand – 3.0 – has been released; key themes for 3.0 are enhancements and optimizations for production deployments and enabling first deployments with a low entry threshold. In particular, all critical component of CloudBand including OpenStack are now fully redundant. With this new release also CloudBand Nodes can be upgraded without downtime, a dramatic improvement for the network management and operations.

For more information, read the CloudBand 3.0 Solution Sheet or watch again Ron Haberman, General Manager for our CloudBand business unit, talking about CloudBand 3.0.
